Oil Painting

My most recent works are the portraits and stamp series, showcasing the development of my oil painting practise. The stamp series was inspired by the concept of Mail Art and the Fluxus movement. The scenes on the stamps are of photographs I took whilst on holiday in the countries included. In the top right hand corner are the coordinates for the location of the scene. If the stamp is in a different location, it ends up at a different set of coordinates, exploring the ease of travel and how this makes the world smaller.
