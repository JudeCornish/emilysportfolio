Life Drawing

These works are from the life drawing sessions I took part in during the first term of my first year at Lancaster University. During these sessions I created studies of the human form using a whole range of different drawing mediums, including tissue paper. These drawings were all produced from direct observation and capture the female body in a range of seated and standing positions.
