Hi, I'm Emily Raymond

My name is Emily and I am currently studying Fine Art and Design at Lancaster University. My work is mainly focused on acrylic and oil painting. My main interest in acrylic painting is exhibited through my use of palette knives which give texture to my work.

While oil painting is a fairly new medium to me, it is a discovery which I intend to pursue as I not only like the flexibility in its slow drying nature, but also the overall finish.

A particular interest, for me, is the combination of oil paint and gold leaf, which I came across in an online Zoom workshop, hosted by the artist Mary Greenacre. She has been a huge inspiration and guide to me in learning about this medium.

I am particularly interested in trying new things and experimentation which is demonstrated through the selection of life drawings. I enjoy exploring the different ways a work can be presented trough changes in colour and texture, which can often be defined by the chosen medium or surface. A lot of my work is from reference images, except for the life drawings, and use of a select few images.

I also use photoshop to create compositions by collaging a combination of reference images. This stage is important to me because it enables me to freely play around with the style and layout of the painting idea before committing it to its physical surface.
