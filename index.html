Oil painting

My Oil paintings are the product of a 2020 lockdown project of trying a new medium. Although I considered it a very traditional painting material, I have discovered there is a whole range of possibilities available within lis medium. Specifically the combination of Oils and Gold leaf: expanding on my interest with textures.
